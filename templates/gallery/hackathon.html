Hackathon on Sustainability, Climatic Change

The United Nations defines sustainability as meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. We, at Waves'24, believe it is so much more! Sustainable development necessitates finding novel solutions to age-old problems: How do we solve the energy crisis? How do we eradicate hunger? How do we protect our natural resources? If you've got answers, join the Waves'24 Sustainability Hackathon! Analyze contemporary global challenges, innovate, experiment and showcase your cutting-edge solutions. Your ideas have the power to change the world for the better!
